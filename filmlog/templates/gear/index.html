Gear

Cameras

You can associate cameras to films (which are part of projects). You can also define lenses to associate with exposures. First add a Camera, then optionally a Lens. Then pull up the Camera details to associated lenses with the camera.

Filters

Filters are associated with exposures. These would include, for example a circular polarizer or a yellow filter. Which filters you add are entirely up to you.

Enlargers

Enlargers and enlarger lenses are associated with darkroom prints.

Holders

If you shoot large format film, you can add the holders you use. This will allow you to track their state (which film is loaded in them, etc.)
